## Lounge Overview

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airport=LHR, terminal=T3
**Summary:** One of the best oneworld lounges at Heathrow with excellent noodle bar and showers.

The Cathay Pacific Lounge at T3 is consistently rated among the best business lounges at Heathrow. Signature features include the famous noodle bar with made-to-order dan dan noodles.

## Access Rules

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airport=LHR, terminal=T3
**Summary:** Open to Cathay business/first, oneworld Sapphire/Emerald on any oneworld flight from T3.

Access is available to:
- Cathay Pacific business and first class passengers
- oneworld Sapphire and Emerald members departing on oneworld carriers
- Marco Polo Club Gold and Diamond members
