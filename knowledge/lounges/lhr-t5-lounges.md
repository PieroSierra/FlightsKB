## Galleries Club Lounge

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airports=LHR, airline=BA
**Summary:** Main BA business class lounge in T5 with dining, bar, and work areas.

The Galleries Club is the primary business class lounge for British Airways at Heathrow T5. It offers:

- Hot and cold buffet dining
- Full bar service
- Shower facilities
- Business center with printing
- Terrace seating with runway views

**Hours:** Opens 5:00 AM, closes with last departure
**Access:** Club World/Club Europe passengers, oneworld Sapphire

## Galleries First Lounge

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airports=LHR, airline=BA
**Summary:** First class lounge with a la carte dining and premium champagne.

The Galleries First lounge offers elevated service including:

- A la carte restaurant service
- Premium champagne selection
- Spa treatments (bookable)
- Private cabanas for rest

**Access:** First class passengers, oneworld Emerald status

## Concorde Room

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airports=LHR, airline=BA
**Summary:** The most exclusive BA lounge with personal service and fine dining.

The Concorde Room is BA's invitation-only lounge offering:

- Private dining with full menu
- Exclusive champagne and wine list
- Private cabanas with daybeds
- Personal shopping service

**Access:** First class on specific routes, BA Gold Guest List members

## Best times to visit

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** airports=LHR
**Summary:** Arrive before 6 AM or after 2 PM to avoid peak crowding.

The Galleries Club lounge is most crowded between 6 AM and 10 AM due to morning long-haul departures. The second peak occurs around 12-2 PM. For the quietest experience, arrive very early or time your visit for mid-afternoon.
