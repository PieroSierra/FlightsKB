## Ex-EU Pricing Advantage

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** regions=EU
**Summary:** Booking from EU points of sale often yields lower fares than UK or US origins.

Airlines price differently based on point of sale. Flights originating from countries like Portugal, Spain, or Poland often have lower base fares than UK departures for the same routing.

## How to Book Ex-EU

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** regions=EU
**Summary:** Use airline websites with EU country/currency settings or OTAs with EU presence.

To access ex-EU fares: use the airline's local website (e.g., ba.com/es), set your browser location to an EU country, or book through EU-based OTAs. Payment method country may also matter.
