## Airlines Offering Free Stopovers

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** type=stopover
**Summary:** Several airlines allow free stopovers at their hub cities on through tickets.

Airlines offering free or low-cost stopovers:
- **Turkish Airlines:** Free Istanbul stopover up to 20 hours with hotel
- **Emirates:** Dubai Connect - free hotel for 8-26 hour layovers
- **Etihad:** Abu Dhabi stopover packages
- **Iberia:** Free Madrid stopover on select fares

## Booking Stopovers

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** type=stopover
**Summary:** Use multi-city search or call the airline to add stopovers.

Free stopovers may not show in standard searches. Use multi-city booking tools or contact the airline directly to add a stopover without increasing the fare.
