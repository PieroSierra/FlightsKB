## Sky Suite Overview

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airline=JL, cabin=business
**Summary:** JAL's award-winning business class with full-flat beds and Japanese hospitality.

Japan Airlines Sky Suite features fully flat beds in a 1-2-1 configuration with direct aisle access. The product is consistently rated among the world's best business class experiences.

## Japanese Service Excellence

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airline=JL, cabin=business
**Summary:** Multi-course Japanese kaiseki meals and premium sake selection available.

JAL business class offers a choice of Japanese kaiseki cuisine or Western dishes. The sake and shochu selection is curated by certified sommeliers. Pajamas are provided on long-haul flights.
