## Airport Overview

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airport=SEA
**Summary:** SEA is a major hub for Alaska Airlines and Delta Air Lines with extensive Pacific routes.

Seattle-Tacoma serves as Alaska Airlines' primary hub and Delta's Pacific gateway. The airport has expanded significantly with international arrivals facility and new gates.

## Lounge Options

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airport=SEA
**Summary:** Multiple lounge options including Alaska, Delta, and Priority Pass lounges.

- **Alaska Lounges:** N and C gates, excellent for Alaska/oneworld flyers
- **Delta Sky Club:** S gates, accessible with Delta status/Amex Platinum
- **The Club at SEA:** Priority Pass accessible
- **Centurion Lounge:** Amex Platinum cardholders
