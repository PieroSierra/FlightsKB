## Terminal Overview

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** airport=LHR
**Summary:** Heathrow has four terminals: T2 (Star Alliance), T3 (oneworld/SkyTeam), T4 (misc), T5 (BA).

- **Terminal 2:** Star Alliance hub (United, Lufthansa, Air Canada, etc.)
- **Terminal 3:** oneworld (AA, CX, QF) and SkyTeam (Virgin Atlantic)
- **Terminal 4:** Smaller carriers, some Middle Eastern airlines
- **Terminal 5:** Exclusively British Airways and Iberia

## Transfer Tips

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** airport=LHR
**Summary:** Allow 90+ minutes for inter-terminal transfers, especially T5 to T2/T3.

Terminals are connected by free trains (Heathrow Express between T5 and T2/T3 area). Security re-screening is required for most transfers. T5 to other terminals takes 20-30 minutes minimum.
