## Status Match Opportunities

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** type=status-match
**Summary:** Many airlines offer status matches or challenges to attract competitors' elite members.

Airlines periodically offer status matches to members of competing programs. These typically require proof of current status and sometimes a minimum spend commitment within 90 days.

## Current Active Matches

**Claim type:** rule_of_thumb
**Applies to:** type=status-match
**Summary:** Check FlyerTalk for current status match offers.

Status match availability changes frequently. Popular ongoing programs include:
- Virgin Atlantic - matches most oneworld/Star Alliance status
- Turkish Airlines - periodic match campaigns
- Emirates - selective matches with spend requirement
