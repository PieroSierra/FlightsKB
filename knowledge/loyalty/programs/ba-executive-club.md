## Tier Structure

**Claim type:** fact
**Applies to:** program=executive-club
**Summary:** Blue, Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Gold Guest List tiers.

BA Executive Club has five tiers earned by collecting Tier Points:
- **Blue:** Entry level, earn and spend Avios
- **Bronze:** 300 TPs - Priority check-in, extra baggage
- **Silver:** 600 TPs - Lounge access (oneworld Sapphire)
- **Gold:** 1500 TPs - First class lounges (oneworld Emerald)
- **Gold Guest List:** 3000 TPs - Concorde Room access, lifetime Gold

## Avios Sweet Spots

**Claim type:** tactic
**Applies to:** program=executive-club
**Summary:** Best value redemptions are short-haul off-peak and partner awards.

Short-haul BA flights in off-peak periods offer excellent value at 4,000-6,000 Avios one-way. Partner awards on Cathay Pacific and Japan Airlines business class often beat BA's own pricing.
